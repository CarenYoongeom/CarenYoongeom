KSMC · Est. 2008 · Suwon, Republic of Korea

Beyond silicon, built for the systems that move the world.

Korean Semiconductor Manufacturing Corporation is a fabless designer of mixed-signal, memory, microcontroller, and power-management ICs for industrial automation, automotive electronics, consumer electronics, and IoT infrastructure. We design in Korea, fabricate in Taiwan and Singapore, and ship under full ISO and AEC-Q100 compliance.

01 / TECHNOLOGY

A full process portfolio, from EUV-class FinFET to mature planar nodes.

KSMC supports designs across seven primary process nodes. EUV lithography at 5nm and 7nm for mobile and high-performance compute; 14nm and 28nm for general-purpose logic and automotive; mature nodes for analog, mixed-signal, and IoT power management.
